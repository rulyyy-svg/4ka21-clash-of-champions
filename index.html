📖 GAMEPLAY GUIDES

Master the rules and play mechanics of our three arena game modes

🎲 1. Snakes & Ladders Quiz Mode

⏱️

Real-Time Turns

Players take turns rolling and moving. The active player has 25 seconds to answer their Information Systems English question.

🎲

Dice Movement

Answering correctly rolls the dice (1 to 6) automatically. An incorrect answer keeps your piece in place.

🐍

Snakes & Ladders

Land on a ladder to climb higher. Land on a snake's head to slide backwards down the board.

🏆

Winning Condition

The first player to reach precisely Tile 100 on the board wins the match and claims the tournament victory.

📝 2. Smart Quiz Mode (Quiz Pintar)

✨

Unique Questions

Each player receives their own set of questions. Your screen will display different questions from other players in the room.

⚡

Speed Bonus Points

Answering correctly awards 50 points plus a bonus based on the remaining timer. Fast answers give huge score boosts!

🔢

20 Rounds Layout

The game consists of exactly 20 rounds of challenging questions. Every player answers concurrently.

🥇

Winning Condition

The player with the highest total score at the end of the 20 rounds wins the match.

🧩 3. Teka Teki Silang (TTS Crossword) Mode

🗺️

Unique Isolated Board

Each player plays on their own 10x10 layout. Across and Down words are isolated to avoid grid collisions.

🎨

Custom Board Sizing

Enjoy custom-rendered wooden crossword board backgrounds that scale fully to the grid container.

⌨️

Interactive Keyboard Inputs

Click any square and type letters directly. Use Arrow keys to navigate the grid or Backspace to delete.

🏁

Winning Condition

Fill all 10 questions correctly and press the Submit button. The fastest player to submit correctly wins!
